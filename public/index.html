CLIMATE MEMORIAL is an exploratory space, containing stories of plants, animals, and other memorialized climate grief. Explore the canvas by clicking on cells to either learn more about a memorial or to add your own -- this can be a favorite tree, a beach, a friend, anything that you would like to memorialize.

Please be respectful of this space and of what others have shared as you enter so that we may share our grief in community.

CLIMATE MEMORIAL was created by Kyle Barnes and Raya Ward and adapted from Permaculture Network by Gary Zhexi Zhang and Agnes Cameron.
